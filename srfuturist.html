Senior Futurist

The Senior Futurist will join NYCx, NYC Tech's Industry Engagement team, which serves as the engine for bold ideas, breakthroughs and the way we interact and influence emerging technologies in the public realm. This role will prepare New York City's tech strategies for the future. You'll collect insights, identify patterns, and design points of view to inform strategy for key projects and initiatives.

As Senior Futurist, you'll research topics like NYC transportation, energy sustainability, zero waste, housing, autonomous vehicles, artificial intelligence, and drone technology, among others. You'll examine trends and breakthroughs in the context of the larger cultural, technological, economic, environmental, and political landscapes, screening for future threats as well as future opportunities.

  Responsibilities

  • Research signals, drivers, and trends that may create uncertainty for topic areas
  • Create a yearly “Moonshot” futures report which includes 10-year-out future scenario(s), drivers of change, gates and flags, and elements to watch
  • Work with the Industry Engagement team and external partners to translate foresight strategy into actionable next steps
  • Monitor and track gates and flags
  • Follow team progress to maintain momentum, assess, and adjust strategies
  • Provide direction and guidance for innovation by blending design with foresight tools
  • Develop and facilitate collaborative foresight workshops and working sessions


  Skills We're Seeking

Government experience, preferably at the municipal level

Results-oriented leadership experience

Experience developing and implementing technology projects from start to finish

Knowledge and experience working with emerging technologies

Can understand the strategic business objectives and translate into strategies, goals, and plans

Can inspire and motivate staff at all levels and maintain effective working relationships with diverse partners in the tech and user community


  Education and Experience

Bachelor's degree and five years' experience of supervisory or administrative experience with a focus on business promotion or urban economic challenges.
