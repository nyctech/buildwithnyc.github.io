Internet of Things (IoT) Engineer

NYC Tech is dedicated to pushing government technology forward to ensure that our systems can scale to future needs and deliver on the promise of Internet of Things (IoT), 5G, and other emerging technology.

The Internet of Things (IoT) Engineer will play a lead role in all things IoT, ranging from design and requirements analysis to investigation of new technologies and software development. You'll engage regularly with senior managers, technologists, and the City’s customers to understand needs and vision and then translate this into an executable plan and roadmap for IoT.


  Responsibilities

  • Provide technical leadership for the development and integration of new IoT technologies and systems
  • Partner with stakeholders to develop overall strategy, policy, and roadmaps for IoT in alignment with the NYC Chief Technology Officer's mission
  • Evaluate and prototype innovative technologies and concepts with IoT and related technologies
  • Help lead comprehensive technical studies, trials, and evaluations of new IoT-related infrastructure, and performance monitoring tools
  • Create policy and strategy for continuous modernization of IoT technology to meet the public’s evolving needs
  • Work with Citywide IT Security team to ensure that IoT technologies meet Citywide security standards and best practices
  • Handle special projects and initiatives as assigned


  Skills We're Seeking

Experience with IoT technologies and systems

Experience advising vendors and staff engineers in prototyping

Knowledge of security frameworks and enterprise architecture for IoT

Well rounded, broad background in web, mobile, and internet technologies

Experience with agile development

Experience with open source code development and APIs


  Education and Experience

Master's degree in computer science with 3 years experience in IT/computer programming, including managerial experience.
